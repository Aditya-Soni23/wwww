ACDC Ltd was founded in the year 2021. It is one of the top 10 chemical companies in India. The company is headquartered in Hyderabad, Telangana It is an Indian multinational company that distribute and markets Pharmaceutical, agrochemicals, industrial chemicals, chemical intermediates, and specialty chemicals. The company also offers best pharmaceutical chemicals requirement solutions. The company is engaged in both Pharma and non- activities. The company’s products are sold in approx 120 countries.
